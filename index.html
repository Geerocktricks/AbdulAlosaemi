EWU Startup Challenge - Report January 28th 2020

DigiCredits

Team Members: Evan Briggs, Matt Dugas, Aziz A.

Pitch Idea: DigiCredits, an online barter exchange with crypto-currency (blockchain) trading

Synopsis: DigiCredits is an online trade platform similar to craigslist. It is Designed to provide the foundation for a marketplace using a proprietary token.

The token, called a DigiCoin, would be used as a medium of exchange between $USD, and other forms of popular crypto currencies and is easier to manage and less energy intensive than regular crypto currency.

Targeted at the gamer market, DigiCoin’s main source of revenue would be gamers exchanging digital goods and providing services on the website to gamers.

Feedback from Judges:

  • Hard to envision application to their everyday lives
  • Confusion on how to get the DigiCoin
  • Confusion on value of the DigiCoin
  • Limited perspectives on gamers exchange market

Result: Did not advance to stage 2

Status: Not under development

EWU Mini Pitch Competition - Report February 6th 2020

Image comes in here!!

Team Members: Evan Briggs, Matt Dugas, Aziz A.

Pitch Idea: Whootels.com, a hotel booking website

Synopsis: Whootels.com

Whootels.com is designed to do what other hotel search engines do not currently provide: Price transparency.

Targeted at travelers, Whootels.com would offer advanced hotel booking filters that show total price information, TPI. This TPI would give an advantage over competitors by providing data that websites such as Expedia.com and the hotel booking sites rely on to earn more profits.

Feedback from Judges:

  • Hard to obtain search rankings on google
  • Don’t agree price transparency has any need

Result: Did not advance to stage 2

Status: See Chapter 2
